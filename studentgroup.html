Student Group Spotlight

Washington University has hundreds of student groups for every interest. From activist organizations to affinity groups to club sport teams WashU has it all. I a group for your interest doesn't exist, it is every easy to start a new club through WU's student government, Student Union.

  • Y's Thoughts: Entrepreneurship and new venture club
  • WUIRC: Model UN and other international relations activities organization
  • Social Programming Board: Puts on Walk In, Lay Down concerts and speakers throughout year
  • Engineers Without Borders: Application of engineering skills to help global communities
