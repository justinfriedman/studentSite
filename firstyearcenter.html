First Year Center Programs

The first year center has programs.....

  • First 40 is a series of fun events and activities on campus and in the community available to new students during their first 40 days at Washington University in St. Louis.

  • Local families are matched with students and provide home-cooked meals several times a year.

  • Students get to know faculty members in a series of relaxed, informal lunches or dinners hosted by the First Year Center.

  • A common reading experience in which a different book is provided to the incoming class each year; during Bear Beginnings and Winter Welcome. Students will have an intellectual discussion in small groups led by a faculty or staff member; additional events are built around this book throughout the year.
