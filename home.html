Get Ready For Four Years Of Fun At Washington University in St. Louis!

Washington University is a diverse and welcoming college enviroment for all new students. The First Year Center helps all students in their first year on campus get aquainted to the city of St. Louis and life on WU campus. WU is a fantastic academic for students devoted to their studies and expantion of their intellectual horizons. Outside of class new students can explore the many cultural and historic areas of the city with their peers.
