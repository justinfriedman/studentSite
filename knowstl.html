Get to Know St. Louis

Useful Spots

St. Louis has a ton of great cultural areas to visit as a new student. Here are a few favorites of current students.

  • The Delmar Loop: Great restaurants and Music
    • Piccione Pastry: Best Bakery on the Loop
    • The Pageant: One of the hottest music venues in St. Louis
    • Mission Taco: Fantastic signature tacos
  • Forest Park
    • Forest Park Zoo: Free zoo for WU students
    • Steinberg Skating Rink: Iceskating in beautiful Forest Park all winter long
    • MUNY: Outdoor public theater
  • Scottrade Center and Downtown
    • St. Louis Blues Hockey: Experiance Midwest Hockey culture at a Blues game
    • Cardinals games at Busch Stadium: Watch one of the best teams in the MLB and enjoy the food in Ballpark Village
    • Gateway Arch: take on St. Louis like a tourist and vist the landmark of the city
  • Target and The Promenade at Brentwood
    • Target: pick up all the odds and ends for your dorm room
    • Micro Center: get any electronic device you possibly need
    • Trader Joe's: great snack food and frozen meals for late night studying
